FAQ

One of my crew members died! How do I get someone new?

Try hanging out at the bar on Planet X. You might run into someone willing to join your party...

What are blorps?

A blorp is a unit commonly used for measuring distance in the 22nd century. There is currently no known way to convert between blorps and clearly inferior 21st century measurements.

How do I get a better score?

Your score is determined by a variety of factors, including the job you started with, how much of your crew survived, and how many resources you have left over when you reach THX-11.
